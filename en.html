The Stone Manuscript

"Welcome, Archaeologist of Time.
You hold in your hands the map of a unique excavation. You won't search for gold or relics, but for something far more valuable: the memory of Barcelona, engraved on its skin of stone."

"Today, your mission is to excavate its secrets. You won't use a pickaxe and shovel, but your wits. Your job will be to remove the dust of centuries to reveal the marks that each civilization left behind."
