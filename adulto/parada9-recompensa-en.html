Epilogue: The Last Layer

BARCELONA! The circle is closed.

The Infinite Manuscript

You have traversed 2000 years of history in less than 2 kilometers: Rome, Visigoths, Al-Andalus, Gothic, Call, Renaissance, Enlightenment, Civil War.

Barcelona is not just stone. It is a palimpsest: a parchment written, erased, and rewritten hundreds of times. Each generation has left its ink over the previous one.

"And today, by walking these streets and rescuing their secrets, you have added your own line to history.

You are the last layer of this manuscript."
