The Legacy of Rome

The Origin (History): The colony Iulia Augusta Faventia Paterna Barcino was founded here in the 1st century BC. The two towers are the Porta Praetoria, the main entrance to the walled city.

The Curiosity (The Compass Letter): Look again at the BARCINO letters on the ground. Take a closer look at the letter 'N'. It's unique for three reasons. It's made of aluminum, not bronze, to shine with the light of the present. Its shape evokes the sail of a ship, in honor of the Mediterranean Sea. And most importantly: the entire letter acts as a compass, pointing North.

Source: MUHBA / Art Públic
