The Sage of the Call

Salomó ben Adret (Rashba): This street bears his name. He was the rabbi of Barcelona for 50 years in the 13th century. His wisdom was so respected that kings and scholars from all over Europe sent him consultations. He was also the moneylender to King James I himself.

The Oldest House: The building at number 6 is considered the oldest inhabited house in Barcelona. Imagine the generations that have crossed that threshold since the 12th century.

Source: MUHBA / Call of Barcelona
