Lights and Shadows (18th Century)

The Traveling House: You have discovered the Padellàs House. Although it is a 15th-century Gothic palace, it was originally on another street. In 1931, to save it from demolition when Via Laietana was opened, it was moved stone by stone to this location.

The End of an Era: You are on Veguer Street. The "Veguer" was the medieval Catalan authority. After the defeat of 1714, King Philip V imposed the New Plant Decree (Decreto de Nueva Planta), eliminating Catalan institutions and imposing the figure of the "Corregidor". This street is a silent reminder of that world which disappeared in the Age of Enlightenment.

Source: MUHBA / Urban History
