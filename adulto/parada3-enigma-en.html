Chapter III: The Invisible Frontier

Plaça Ramon Berenguer · Al-Andalus

“You arrive at the scar of an ancient frontier. For almost a century, the Llobregat river, a few kilometers from here, separated the Carolingian world from Al-Andalus. Barcelona was not a Muslim city, but a Christian border stronghold living under its shadow and influence.”

“To understand this era, we will not look for a mosque, but for the statue of the man who dedicated his life to fighting on that frontier: Ramon Berenguer III, the Great, one of the first counts to begin the expansion to the south.”

⚔️

The Count's Code

“Read the full name of the Count on horseback engraved on the plaque carefully. Hidden among his letters and title, there are two Roman numerals. Add them together to find the solution.”

Look for a letter in his first name (RAMON) that is also a high-value Roman numeral. Then, look at the number that follows his name at the end.
