Before You Start

  • YOUR MISSION Solve a series of historical riddles using your wits and the city itself. The adventure takes about 90 to 120 minutes.
  • PUZZLE HINTS If you get stuck on a puzzle, don't worry! After 3 wrong guesses, a hint button will appear to help you out.
  • GETTING AROUND To get from one stop to the next, you'll get a clue. If that's not enough, you can unlock a Direct Clue by passing a small attention test, and if you're still lost, another test will unlock a Map link.
  • PLAY YOUR WAY... OR COMPETE! Enjoy the walk and explore at your own pace... or challenge your friends to see who can decipher the manuscript first!
