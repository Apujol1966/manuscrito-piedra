Before You Start

  • YOUR MISSION Solve a series of historical riddles using the surroundings and your wits. The adventure lasts between 90 and 120 minutes. The game starts at Plaça de Catalunya.
  • RIDDLE HINTS If you get stuck on a puzzle, don't worry! After 3 failed attempts, a button will appear to help you.
  • NAVIGATION HELP To get from one stop to another, we'll give you a hint. If that's not enough, by passing small attention tests, you can unlock a Direct Hint, and if it's still not enough, another hint with a Map.
  • PLAY AT YOUR PACE... OR COMPETE! Enjoy the walk at your own pace... or challenge your friends to see who deciphers the manuscript first!
